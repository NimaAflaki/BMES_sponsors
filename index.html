Sponsorship Details for Biomaterials Day 2026

Partner with BMES/SFB Chapter at the University of Memphis to advance biomedical engineering education and innovation

Biomaterials Day is a full-day conference at the FedEx Institute of Technology bringing together students, researchers, and professionals to celebrate biomaterials innovation. The event features a keynote speaker, 3-Minute Thesis competitions, guest faculty speakers, poster sessions, a seminar, and networking opportunities throughout the day including meals, workshops, and an evening awards ceremony.

Why Sponsor Biomaterials Day?

Sponsoring Biomaterials Day connects your company with ~100 students, researchers, and faculty—positioning you as a leader in advancing biomaterials innovation.
